Hunted

Mateusz, Thomas

WIT, 2nd year Game Design project

Art by AL Tseng (阿麟),found here.

Welcome to our website for our game design project. It's a competetive 1v2 assymmetrical board game where one side playing the demons is trying to complete objectives around a 20x20 board and escape from the demon hunter trying to stop them. The game includes an element of guesswork as not all objectives count towards the demons' win condition, and only the demons know where the important objectives are.
